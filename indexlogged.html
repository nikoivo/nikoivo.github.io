Hearthstone - the Newest and Best in Pay-to-Win!

Hearthstone is Blizzard's newest attempt at getting in on the free-to-play market. It's a fairly casual, card-based game in which each player takes turns playing cards from their hand. These cards will have various effects, and are used to take your opponent down to 0 health, winning the game. It starts out fairly simply. You're thrust into a tutorial several missions long to get the hang of the game. The AI throws out some comical dialogue, and you learn while you laugh at it a bit. The gameplay is very easy to learn, and while it may seem a bit two-dimensional to trading card game veterans, it still has a lot to offer. Once you beat the tutorial, you have the option of choosing to battle against one of 9 different classes, unlocking each upon beating it. Each one has a set of cards unique to it, along with many that can be used by any class. Each class also has a distinct play style; a method of victory that they excel at.
